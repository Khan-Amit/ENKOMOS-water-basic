# 💧 ENKOMOS-Water-Basic

**Structured Water Intelligence | Offline | Encrypted | Tier-Based**

---

## Purpose

ENKOMOS-Water-Basic is a mobile-ready, offline-first web application for monitoring and managing structured water quality in aquaculture, irrigation, and greenhouse systems.

It is designed for three grower tiers:

| Tier | User Type | Size | Focus |
|------|-----------|------|-------|
| **A** | Home / Small Grower | <0.5 hectare | Rooftop, backyard, balcony, small garden |
| **B** | Co-op / Medium Commercial | 0.5-2 hectares | Collective farms, cooperative growers |
| **C** | Corporate / Large Commercial | 2+ hectares | Large farms, agribusiness, estates |

---

## What It Does

- ✅ **Record water quality** (pH, ORP, dissolved oxygen, temperature, TDS, EC, ammonia, nitrite)
- ✅ **Track structured water methods** (magnetic coil, vortex chamber, electric induction, hybrid)
- ✅ **Monitor fish health** (tilapia, prawn, carp, clams, octopus)
- ✅ **Log aeration equipment** (aerators, pumps, air stones, fountains)
- ✅ **Store data offline** with AES-256 encryption
- ✅ **Export encrypted files** for analysis by ENKOMOS Main (Python version)
- ✅ **Provide AI advice** based on latest readings
- ✅ **Tier-specific structured water guides**

---

## How It Works

1. Open the app (works offline after first load)
2. Select your tier (A, B, or C)
3. Enter water quality readings (manual from sensors or lab)
4. Save — data is encrypted and stored on your device
5. Export `.enc` file and send to ENKOMOS Main for advanced analysis

---

## Data Security

- All data is encrypted locally using AES-256
- No cloud storage unless you choose to export
- Your data never leaves your device without your action
- Exported files are encrypted and can only be opened by ENKOMOS Main
